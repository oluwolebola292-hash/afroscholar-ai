Study Visa Requirements & Interview Prep

Required Documents

Essential documents for European study visa applications:

  • Valid Passport - Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your stay
  • University Admission Letter - Official acceptance from your chosen institution
  • Proof of Financial Means - Bank statements, scholarship letters, or sponsor documents
  • Health Insurance - Coverage for the duration of your stay
  • Academic Transcripts - Certified copies of previous educational records
  • Language Proficiency - IELTS, TOEFL, or relevant language certificate
  • Motivation Letter - Statement of purpose for your studies
  • Accommodation Proof - Confirmation of housing arrangements

Interview Preparation

Practice common visa interview questions:

Why do you want to study in Europe?

Focus on academic opportunities, quality of education, and career goals.

How will you finance your studies?

Be specific about funding sources: savings, scholarships, family support.

What are your plans after graduation?

Demonstrate intent to return home or clear career path.

Why this specific university and course?

Show research about the program, faculty, and opportunities.
